Project Coordinators

A Project Coordinator ensures that BeMSA Ghent projects are well organized and run smoothly. They oversee the planning and management of projects, assign tasks to team members, coordinate communication, and keep track of progress. They also contact sponsors and external partners to support project activities and make sure everything stays on schedule and in good hands.
